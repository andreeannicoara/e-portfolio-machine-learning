# Unit 1

## Reflection
### What?
Unit 1 introduced the foundations of machine learning and clarified the structure of the module, including the later shift into deep learning and the main assessment components. It also covered core concepts such as the difference between software engineering and machine learning, the role of big data, and the main categories of machine learning. 
### So what?
This unit gave me a clearer starting point for the rest of the module. I understood that machine learning is not only about producing predictions, but also about training, testing, generalization, and the quality of the underlying data. I also found it useful that the content linked machine learning to wider industrial and social contexts, because this made the subject feel practical rather than purely theoretical. 
### Now what?
Going forward, I want to build on this foundation by keeping a consistent weekly workflow through notes and reflections. Unit 1 showed me that structured engagement will help me understand later technical content more effectively and produce stronger evidence for my e-portfolio.
